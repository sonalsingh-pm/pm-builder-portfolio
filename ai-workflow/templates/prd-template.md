## TEMPLATE USAGE NOTES
> This template is designed to produce world class PRDs. Before writing, answer these questions:
> 1. What is the single most important problem this product solves?
> 2. Who specifically feels this pain most acutely?
> 3. What is the smallest possible MVP that proves the core hypothesis?
> 4. What is the hardest problem in this product — and what is your honest plan for it?
> 5. How does this product make money?
> Remove all template notes (lines starting with >) before sharing externally.
