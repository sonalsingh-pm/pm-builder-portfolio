# Rejected Patterns — What Did Not Work

These approaches were tried and failed. Do not retest them.

## Rejected 001
**Pattern:** Including timeline and milestones inside a PRD.
**Why rejected:** Timelines belong in project plans.
Baking them into a PRD makes the document stale the moment
a sprint slips. Separating them keeps the PRD durable.
**Date:** May 2026
